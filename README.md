# Olist E-commerce Analytics: Customer, Operations & Revenue

End-to-end analysis of 99K+ orders from Olist, a Brazilian e-commerce 
marketplace. Covers revenue trends, customer segmentation (RFM), 
delivery operations, and prioritized business recommendations.

Live Tableau Dashboard: *(coming Sunday May 11)*  
Walkthrough video: *(coming Sunday May 11)*

## Dataset
- Source: Olist on Kaggle (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/olistbr/brazilian-ecommerce)
- Scope: 99,441 orders | 96,096 customers | 3,095 sellers | Sep 2016 to Sep 2018
- Tables: 9

## Business Questions
1. Which product categories and regions drive the most revenue?
2. How is monthly revenue trending?
3. Who are our most valuable customer segments (RFM)?
4. Which sellers and regions have the worst delivery performance?
5. Does delivery delay hurt customer satisfaction (review scores)?

## Stack
